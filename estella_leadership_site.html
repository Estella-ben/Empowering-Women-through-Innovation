Problem & Solution

The Problem

Women continue to be significantly underrepresented in leadership roles across many industries, especially in technology and entrepreneurship. Systemic biases, lack of mentorship, and limited access to leadership training often hinder their advancement.

Supporting Data:

üìä Only 28% of managerial positions worldwide are held by women (UN Women, 2024)
üåç In Africa, women make up less than 20% of executive positions in tech

My Solution

I'm developing an interactive, digital mentorship and resource hub that supports emerging women leaders. This platform connects users with mentors, provides leadership training modules, hosts discussion forums, and highlights success stories to inspire and empower.

How it works:

  • Users create profiles to access tailored resources
  • They can be matched with mentors in their field
  • They complete goal-oriented leadership challenges
  • Community forums encourage support and shared learning
